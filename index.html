About LiveCVEBench

LiveCVEBench is a continuously updated benchmark for evaluating Large Language Models (LLMs) on real-world CVE (Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures) vulnerability detection and patching tasks.

Unlike static benchmarks that may suffer from data contamination, LiveCVEBench sources new CVEs after model training cutoff dates, ensuring a fair and unbiased evaluation of model capabilities.
